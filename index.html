Tri-Cities travel lacrosse team

Competitive, summer travel lacrosse for high school boys in the Tri-Cities looking to improve their game.

Who We Are

Our coaching staff is made up of coaches from Science Hill High School and Kingsport teams. We've gotten to see how talented young men in the Tri-Cities are and thought it was a shame they didn't have more opportunities to get in front of college coaches. We wanted to create a team for all those motivated, hard-working student athletes who want to take their lacrosse game to the next level.

Because we're just starting out we will have one team comprised of upcoming 9th-12th graders, and will eventually have a team for each grade. As of now we are expecting to have 1-2 practices each week in the summer and have signed on for the tournaments you see below. Our tryouts will be April 11th, and April 18th at Colonial Heights Middle School. Once the time is decided, it will be posted on this site as well as emailed to all of you. Thank you for your interest and we hope to see you this summer!
